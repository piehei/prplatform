Questions are generic and support three types: textual answer in a text box, choices, and file upload.
Each of the three types are accompanied by a question text (the actual question or short instruction).
Types cannot be mixed, ie. one question can only be of one type but one peer-review exercise can have multiple questions.

For textual answer, leave choices and filetypes empty. For choices, fill in the choices according to the instructions at the bottom of the page and leave filetypes empty. For file upload, leave choices empty and fill in the filetypes. Always fill in the question text.
